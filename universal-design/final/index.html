What is Universal Design?

Universal design is the practice of creating websites that are accessible and user-friendly for all people, regardless of their abilities, devices, or preferences. A universally designed website ensures that everyone can navigate, understand, and interact with it effectively.

By applying universal design principles, you make the internet a more inclusive space for people with disabilities, the elderly, and even those who might be using assistive technologies. Universal design isn't just about accessibilityâ€”it's about enhancing the user experience for all.

Why Universal Design Matters

More than just a legal requirement in many regions, universal design leads to better usability, wider reach, and greater customer satisfaction. It ensures your website is usable by anyone, anywhere, at any time.

By prioritizing accessibility, you tap into a larger audience and demonstrate a commitment to inclusivity.
